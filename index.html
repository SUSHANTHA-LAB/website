About the journal


International Journal of Computational and Experimental Research (IJCER) is an open access, and peer-reviewed International Journal. IJCER provides a platform for the researchers to publish and share their knowledge in the form of high quality empirical and theoretical research papers, technical report, case studies, literature reviews and book reviews both in computational and experimental works. The Journal is published in online version. Authors are responsible about article’s content and consequences. All papers will be checked for plagiarism and the authenticate tool is used for plagiarism screening.IJCER does NOT charge any article submission or processing fee.

International Journal of Computational and Experimental Research (IJCER) aims to be an international platform to exchange novel research results in simulation-based science across all scientific disciplines. It publishes advanced innovative, interdisciplinary research where complex multi-scale, multi-domain problems in science and engineering are solved, integrating sophisticated numerical methods, computation, data, networks, and novel devices. The journal welcomes original, unpublished high-quality contributions in the field of computational science at large, addressing one or more of the aforementioned elements.


AIM


The main aim of IJCER to provide an international scientific platform for Authors-readers- researcher to share their works. Authors are responsible about article’s content and consequences. IJCER has all the copyrights of articles submitted to be published. IJCER provides an international medium for the communication of original research, ideas and developments.



Scope:


IJCER provides an international medium for the communication of original research, ideas and developments in the field of Natural Sciences-Engineering-Technology includes both theoretical- computational and experimental works.
